About Inkwell Bookstore

A sanctuary for readers, dreamers, story-seekers, and lovers of Jamaican literature.

Our Story

Inkwell Bookstore was born from a love of storytelling and the belief that books can shape minds, spark imagination, and inspire personal growth. It began as a small corner shop in 1992 and has grown into a beloved landmark.

We proudly celebrate Jamaican and Caribbean authors, while also offering a diverse collection of global literature — from novels and poetry to academic texts and rare finds. We believe in the power of the written word to connect us all.

More than a bookstore, Inkwell is a community hub: a quiet space to study, explore ideas, and discover new worlds between pages. We host monthly readings, children's story times, and literary workshops.
